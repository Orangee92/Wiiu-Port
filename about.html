About Me

Hello My name is Everett, and I am a visual storyteller driven by curiosity, creativity, and a love for design. With an Associate's in Animation & Motion Graphics and a Bachelor's in Communication Design, I like to bring stories to life through motion, branding, and immersive visuals.

I specialize in 2D/3D animation, branding, and interactive design using tools like Cinema 4D, Adobe Creative Suite, Unreal Engine, and iClone. I work as a mentor and tutor, helping others grow while continuing to refine my own craft.

This portfolio is styled after the Wii menu to reflect my love for gaming, creative tech, and accessible design. Dive in and explore the channels — each one is a part of my creative journey.
